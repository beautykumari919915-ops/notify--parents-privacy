Privacy Policy — Notify Parents

Notify Parents is a parental control application designed to help parents monitor and protect their children’s digital safety.

Information We Collect

  • Device information (battery status, online status)
  • Location data for safety tracking
  • Notification content from selected apps
  • App usage statistics
  • Account information via Google Sign-In

How We Use Information

Information is used to provide real-time safety alerts and device monitoring for parents.

Notification Access

This app uses Android's Notification Listener Service to monitor notifications with user consent.

Location Data

Location is used only for safety tracking and shared only with linked parent accounts.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data.

Transparency

A persistent notification indicates monitoring is active.

Children’s Privacy

This app is intended for parental use with appropriate consent.
